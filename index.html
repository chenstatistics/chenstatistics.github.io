Research Interests
  • Statistical machine learning and dimension reduction for precision medicine; heterogeneous treatment effect estimation
  • Statistical methods and large-scale data analysis for cancer genomic and genetic data
  • Causal inference: causal effects estimation for high-dimensional regression
  • Bioinformatics and computational biology for big data of cancer
  • Statistical analysis for randomized numerical algorithms
  • Applications to biomedical science and clinical trials
